## 📖 Overview

This document provides the **complete syllabus** for the **TCS Ignite Hiring Assessment**. The assessment evaluates candidates in four major areas:

- 🔢 Numerical Ability
- 🧠 Logical Reasoning
- 📝 Verbal Ability
- 💻 Coding Ability

> **Note**
>
> The official TCS syllabus does not prescribe every individual subtopic. The topics listed below are compiled from the official test pattern, the official sample paper, and recurring topics observed across recent TCS Ignite/Smart hiring assessments. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
