# General Questions

## Q1. What is TCS Ignite?

TCS Ignite is a career program designed for science graduates (BCA, B.Sc., and B.Voc.) that combines recruitment with structured learning. Selected candidates receive professional training before being deployed to projects.

---

## Q2. What is the difference between TCS Ignite and TCS Smart Hiring?

TCS conducts an integrated hiring process for BCA/B.Sc./B.Voc. graduates. Based on your performance, you may be considered for different career tracks such as **Ignite** or **Smart**, depending on the hiring cycle and company requirements.

---

## Q3. Is TCS Ignite only for freshers?

Yes.

The program is primarily intended for eligible fresh graduates from the specified academic batches announced in the official notification.

---

## Q4. Is there any application fee?

No.

TCS does **not** charge any application or registration fee.

If anyone asks for money for recruitment, treat it as fraudulent.

# Registration

## Q9. Where do I register?

Register through the official **TCS NextStep** portal and complete your application before the deadline.

---

## Q10. I cannot find the "Apply for Drive" button.

Try the following:

- Complete every mandatory profile section.
- Log out and log in again.
- Ensure you've registered under the correct hiring category.
- Verify your qualification and graduation year.

If the issue continues, contact TCS support through the official portal.

---

## Q11. Can I edit my application after submission?

Some profile details can be updated before the deadline, while others may be locked after submission.

Always review your application carefully before submitting.

---

# Assessment

## Q12. What is the exam pattern?

The assessment duration is **120 minutes** and includes four sections:

| Section | Duration |
|----------|----------|
| Numerical Ability | 30 Minutes |
| Verbal Ability | 30 Minutes |
| Reasoning Ability | 30 Minutes |
| Coding (Optional) | 30 Minutes |

---

## Q13. Is there negative marking?

No.

There is **no negative marking** for incorrect answers.

---

## Q14. Can I switch between sections?

No.

Each section has its own fixed time limit.

Once a section ends, you cannot return to it.

---

## Q15. Is the test online?

The assessment is conducted by **TCS iON**.

The official notification will specify whether your test is conducted at a test center or another approved mode.

---

## Q16. Will I get rough paper?

TCS provides the resources specified in the official assessment instructions.

Read the latest exam guidelines before test day.

---

# Coding Round

## Q17. Is coding compulsory?

No.

The Coding section is **optional**.

However, attempting it can strengthen your overall profile and is recommended if you're comfortable with programming.

---

## Q18. Which programming languages are supported?

The supported languages typically include:

- C
- C++
- Java
- Python
- Perl

Always check the official sample paper or assessment instructions for the latest supported languages.

---

## Q19. Should I attempt the coding question?

Yes.

If you know programming, you should attempt it.

Even partial solutions can demonstrate problem-solving ability.

---

## Q20. What type of coding questions are asked?

Common topics include:

- Strings
- Arrays
- Loops
- Functions
- Searching
- Sorting
- Basic Data Structures
- Pattern Problems

---

# Interview

## Q21. When will interviews be conducted?

Only shortlisted candidates are invited for interviews.

Interview invitations are sent to your registered email address.

---

## Q22. What is asked in the interview?

The interview may include:

- Self Introduction
- Project Discussion
- Programming Basics
- DBMS
- SQL
- OOP
- HR Questions
- Problem Solving
- Communication Skills

---

## Q23. Is the interview difficult?

Not necessarily.

Interviewers generally focus on your fundamentals, communication skills, confidence, and understanding of your projects.

---

The usual process is:

Assessment

↓

Shortlisting

↓

Interview

↓

Offer Letter

↓

Training

↓

Project Allocation

---

## Q25. Will I receive training?

Yes.

Selected candidates receive training as part of the onboarding process before being assigned to projects.

---

## Q26. Can I grow after joining Ignite?

Yes.

TCS provides structured learning opportunities, certifications, and career progression based on your performance and business requirements.

# Preparation

It depends on your current level.

| Preparation Time | Recommendation |
|-----------------|---------------|
| 60 Days | Excellent |
| 45 Days | Very Good |
| 30 Days | Good |
| 15 Days | Intensive Preparation |
| 7 Days | Revision Only |

---

## Q28. Which section is the most important?

Every section matters.

Focus especially on:

- Numerical Ability
- Logical Reasoning
- Verbal Ability
- Coding (if you know programming)

---

## Q29. Which language should I choose for coding?

Choose the language you're most comfortable with.

For many BCA and B.Sc. students, **Python** is a practical choice because of its readability and concise syntax.

---

## Q30. Are previous-year questions useful?

Yes.

They help you understand:

- Question patterns
- Difficulty level
- Frequently tested topics
- Time management

However, don't rely only on memorizing previous questions.
