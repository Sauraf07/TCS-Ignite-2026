# 📅 Before the Test

## One Day Before

- ✅ Revise important formulas instead of learning new topics.
- ✅ Practice one final mock test.
- ✅ Download and print your Hall Ticket (if provided).
- ✅ Verify your test centre location.
- ✅ Arrange a valid government-issued photo ID.
- ✅ Charge your mobile phone (for navigation only).
- ✅ Sleep for at least 7–8 hours.
- ✅ Keep your documents ready.

> **Pro Tip**
>
> Avoid studying late into the night. A fresh mind performs better than an exhausted one.

---

# 🎒 What to Carry

Carry only the essentials.

| Item | Required |
|-------|:-------:|
| Admit Card / Hall Ticket (if issued) | ✅ |
| Government Photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Driving License, Voter ID) | ✅ |
| Passport-size Photograph (if requested) | ✅ |
| Water Bottle (if allowed) | ✅ |
| Transparent Folder | Optional |

---

# 🚫 What NOT to Carry

The following items are generally prohibited inside the examination hall.

- Mobile Phone
- Smart Watch
- Calculator
- Earphones
- Bluetooth Devices
- USB Drives
- Notes
- Books
- Printed Material
- Electronic Gadgets
- Bags (unless permitted)

> **Note**
>
> Follow the instructions provided in your admit card and by the test centre staff, as permitted items may vary by location.

---

# ⏰ Reporting Time

Arrive at least **30–45 minutes before** your reporting time.

Early arrival gives you enough time for:

- Document Verification
- Identity Verification
- Seat Allocation
- Security Check
- Biometric Verification (if applicable)

Avoid arriving late, as late entry may not be permitted.

---

# 👔 Dress Code

There is no official dress code.

Recommended attire:

- Comfortable clothes
- Comfortable footwear
- Avoid excessive accessories
- Carry a light jacket if the testing room is air-conditioned

Professional dressing creates a positive impression if interviews are conducted on the same day.

---

# 🏢 At the Test Centre

Once you arrive:

1. Complete document verification.
2. Follow the instructions given by the invigilator.
3. Locate your assigned computer.
4. Read all instructions carefully before starting.
5. Ask questions before the timer begins if anything is unclear.

---

# 💻 During the Examination

The TCS Ignite assessment is conducted on the **TCS iON platform** at designated test centres. The assessment duration is **120 minutes**, divided into four sections:

| Section | Duration |
|----------|---------:|
| Numerical Ability | 30 Minutes |
| Verbal Ability | 30 Minutes |
| Reasoning Ability | 30 Minutes |
| Coding (Optional) | 30 Minutes |

Each section has its own allotted time. Read the on-screen instructions carefully before beginning. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

# ⏱️ Time Management Strategy

## Numerical Ability

- Solve easy questions first.
- Skip lengthy calculations initially.
- Return to difficult questions if time permits.

Target Time:

- Easy Questions → 1 minute
- Medium Questions → 1.5 minutes
- Difficult Questions → Skip and revisit

---

## Verbal Ability

Start with:

- Vocabulary
- Grammar
- Error Detection

Finish with:

- Reading Comprehension

---

## Reasoning

Recommended order:

1. Coding-Decoding
2. Series
3. Blood Relations
4. Direction Sense
5. Syllogism
6. Seating Arrangement
7. Puzzles

Avoid spending too much time on a single puzzle.

---

# 💻 Coding Round Tips

Although the coding section is optional, attempting it can strengthen your overall performance.

Recommended approach:

- Read the problem twice.
- Understand all constraints.
- Consider edge cases.
- Write clean and simple code.
- Test with sample inputs.
- Check for syntax errors before submission.

Supported programming languages include:

- Python
- Java
- C
- C++
- Perl

Refer to the official sample paper for the supported language list. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

# ❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Arriving late
- Forgetting your ID proof
- Not reading instructions
- Spending too much time on one question
- Panicking after a difficult question
- Ignoring coding practice
- Leaving questions unanswered without a strategy
- Rushing through calculations
- Clicking options without reading carefully

---

# ⚠️ If You Face Technical Issues

Immediately inform the invigilator if you experience:

- Computer freeze
- Keyboard malfunction
- Mouse issues
- Screen problems
- Login issues
- Network interruption

Do **not** attempt to fix technical problems yourself.

---

# ✅ After Completing the Test

Before leaving:

- Review your answers if time allows.
- Ensure your coding solution has been submitted (if attempted).
- Follow the invigilator's instructions.
- Log out only when instructed.
- Collect your belongings.
