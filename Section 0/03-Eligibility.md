# 🎓 TCS Ignite Eligibility Criteria

> **Last Updated:** July 2026  
> **Source:** Official TCS Careers Portal

---

# 📖 Overview

Before investing your time in preparation, make sure you meet the eligibility requirements for the **TCS Ignite Hiring Process**.

TCS verifies eligibility at multiple stages of the recruitment process. Any incorrect information or failure to meet the eligibility criteria may result in disqualification.

> **Note**
>
> Eligibility criteria may vary slightly across hiring batches. Always verify the latest notification on the official TCS Careers portal before applying.

# 📚 Eligible Courses

Candidates must have completed a **3-year full-time undergraduate degree** in one of the following programs.

## ✅ BCA

- Bachelor of Computer Applications

---

## ✅ B.Sc.

Eligible specializations include:

- Information Technology (IT)
- Computer Science (CS)
- Mathematics
- Data Science
- Statistics
- Physics
- Chemistry
- Electronics
- Cybersecurity
- Biochemistry

---

## ✅ B.Voc.

Eligible specialization:

- Computer Science
- Information Technology (IT)

---

# 📅 Year of Passing

Candidates must belong to the hiring batch announced by TCS.

For the current Ignite drive, eligible batches include:

- 2026 Pass Out

Some combined Ignite & Smart hiring drives may also include the previous graduating batch depending on the official notification.

---

# 📊 Academic Percentage Criteria

Candidates must have

✅ Minimum **50% aggregate** (or equivalent CGPA) in

- Class 10
- Class 12
- Diploma (if applicable)
- Graduation

The graduation percentage is calculated using **all semesters/years**.

---

# 📖 CGPA Conversion

If your university awards CGPA instead of percentage,

Use the conversion formula prescribed by your university.

If no official conversion exists, TCS generally follows the university's declared conversion method.

---

# 📚 Backlog Criteria

Backlog rules are important.

### At the time of applying

- Follow the latest drive notification.

### At the time of joining TCS

You must have

- No active backlogs
- Successfully completed all semesters
- Cleared every subject within the prescribed course duration

Failure to clear pending backlogs before joining can lead to cancellation of the offer.

---

# ⏳ Education Gap

Educational gaps are allowed only within the limits specified by TCS.

General guidelines include:

- Maximum overall academic gap of **24 months (2 years)**
- Every education gap must be declared honestly in the application form
- Documentary proof may be required during verification

Examples of valid reasons include:

- Medical reasons
- Competitive exam preparation
- Personal circumstances
- Family responsibilities

---

# 🏫 Course Type

Eligible

- ✅ Full-time courses

Not Eligible

- ❌ Part-time courses
- ❌ Correspondence courses
- ❌ Distance education (unless specifically mentioned by TCS)

Candidates who completed Class 10 or Class 12 through **NIOS** are generally eligible, provided higher education qualifications are full-time.

---

# 🎂 Age Criteria

Typical eligibility:

- Minimum Age: **18 Years**
- Maximum Age: **28 Years**

Always refer to the official notification for the applicable hiring cycle.

---

# 💼 Work Experience

TCS Ignite is designed primarily for **entry-level graduates**.

Candidates with prior internships or limited work experience may still apply if the hiring notification permits.

If you have work experience,

- Mention it accurately in the application form.
- Keep supporting documents ready for verification.

# ❌ Disqualification Conditions

Your application may be rejected if:

- False information is provided.
- Academic details do not match official documents.
- Minimum percentage criteria are not met.
- Active backlogs remain at the time of joining.
- Required documents cannot be produced during verification.
- The application form is incomplete or inaccurate.

---

# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## Can BCA students apply?

✅ Yes.

---

## Can B.Sc. students apply?

Yes, if your specialization is listed in the eligible disciplines.

---

## Can final-year students apply?

Yes, if the official notification includes your year of passing and you meet all other eligibility conditions.

---

## Is 49.9% eligible?

No.

The minimum aggregate requirement is **50% or equivalent CGPA**.

---

## Are diploma students eligible?

Diploma marks are considered if applicable, but the qualifying undergraduate degree must also meet the eligibility criteria.

---

## Can candidates with education gaps apply?

Yes, provided the total academic gap is within the allowed limit and is declared honestly.

---

## Are correspondence degrees accepted?

No.

Only full-time undergraduate courses are eligible unless the official notification explicitly states otherwise.
