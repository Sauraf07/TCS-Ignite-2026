# Why is Number Series Important?

Number Series is one of the easiest scoring topics in TCS Ignite.

Usually these questions require you to:

• Find the next number
• Find the missing number
• Find the wrong number
• Identify the rule

Most questions can be solved within 20–40 seconds after enough practice.

---

# What is a Number Series?

A Number Series is simply a sequence of numbers arranged according to a specific mathematical rule.

Example:

2 4 6 8 10 ?

Here every number increases by 2.

Answer:
12

---

# Real-Life Example

Imagine climbing stairs.

Floor numbers are:

1
2
3
4
5

Every step increases by one.

This is also a series.
