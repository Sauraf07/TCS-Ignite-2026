# What is a Part of Speech?

Every English sentence is made up of words.

Each word has a specific job.

These jobs are called **Parts of Speech**.

Think of a sentence as a cricket team.

Every player has a different role.

Similarly,

- Noun → Player
- Pronoun → Substitute Player
- Verb → Action
- Adjective → Quality
- Adverb → How
- Preposition → Position
- Conjunction → Connector
- Interjection → Emotion

There are **8 Parts of Speech**

1. Noun
2. Pronoun
3. Verb
4. Adjective
5. Adverb
6. Preposition
7. Conjunction
8. Interjection

Today we will study the first two.

# What is a Noun?

A **Noun** is the name of

- Person
- Place
- Animal
- Thing
- Idea

## Formula

Noun = Name

Examples

Rahul

Delhi

Computer

Dog

School

Book

Honesty

Love

---

# Real-Life Example

Imagine you enter your classroom.

You see:

Teacher

Students

Bench

Fan

Projector

Notebook

All of these are names.

Therefore, they are **Nouns**.

Specific names.

Mumbai

## 2. Common Noun

General names.

## 3. Collective Noun

Name of a group.

Names of materials.

Examples

Iron

---

## 5. Abstract Noun

Cannot be seen or touched.

Examples

Honesty

Bravery

Kindness

Freedom

Knowledge

Sentence

Honesty is the best policy.

# What is a Pronoun?

A **Pronoun** is a word used instead of a noun.

Why?

Because repeating the same noun again and again sounds awkward.

Example

❌ Rahul is a student.
Rahul studies at DAVV.
Rahul likes coding.

✅ Rahul is a student.
He studies at DAVV.
He likes coding.

"He" replaces Rahul.

"He" is a Pronoun.

Example

---

## Demonstrative Pronouns

That

These

Example

---

## Interrogative Pronouns

Who

Whom

Whose

Which

What

Example

---

## Relative Pronouns

Who

Whom

Which

That

Whose

Example

---

## Indefinite Pronouns

Someone

Anyone

Nobody

Everything

Example

# Common TCS Mistakes

❌ Rahul and me went to school.

✅ Rahul and I went to school.

---

❌ Me is a student.

✅ I am a student.

---

❌ Him is my brother.

✅ He is my brother.

# Practice Questions

## Identify the Noun

### Q1

Rahul is reading a book.

A. is

B. reading

C. Rahul

D. a

Answer:

C

---

### Q2

The dog barked loudly.

A. loudly

B. barked

C. dog

D. the

Answer:

C

---

### Q3

Honesty is the best policy.

A. Honesty

B. best

C. policy

D. is

Answer:

A

---

### Q4

The army defeated the enemy.

Which word is the Collective Noun?

A. defeated

B. army

C. enemy

D. the

Answer:

B

---

### Q5

Gold is expensive.

Which type of noun is Gold?

A. Proper

B. Material

C. Common

D. Abstract

Answer:

B
