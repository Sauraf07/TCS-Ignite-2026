# 📚 What is a Number?

A number is a mathematical value used for counting, measuring, and performing calculations.

Real-life examples:

- ₹250 in your wallet
- 45 students in a class
- 98% battery on your phone

All of these are numbers.

---

# 💡 Shortcut

For two numbers

HCF × LCM = Product of Numbers

Example

12 and 18

HCF = 6

LCM = 36

6 × 36 = 216

12 × 18 = 216

Verified.

# ⭐ TCS Tips

Remember:

✔ 2 is the only even prime number.

✔ 1 is neither prime nor composite.

✔ Every prime number has exactly two factors.

✔ Every composite number has more than two factors.

✔ Factors are finite.

✔ Multiples are infinite.

✔ HCF is always smaller than or equal to the numbers.

✔ LCM is always greater than or equal to the numbers.
